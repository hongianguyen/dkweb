About Our Team

Led by 20 Years of Industry Excellence

DKC FLORENO is a premier flooring contractor led by Mr. DK Cho (Dongkyu). With over two decades of hands-on experience in the flooring industry, Mr. Cho brings a level of precision and artistry that is rare in the field.

Our professional team has successfully managed and executed critical projects for commercial sites across Winnipeg and Vancouver Island. We take pride in our extensive portfolio of government contracts, where strict adherence to quality standards and timelines is paramount.

Our Services

From structural preparation to artistic finishing, we cover the full spectrum of commercial flooring needs.

Floor Preparation

Professional floor leveling and tapering to ensure a perfectly smooth substrate for any installation.

Installation Specialists

Expert installation of Sheet Vinyl, Linoleum, Rubber flooring, Carpet Tiles, and Luxury Vinyl Tile (LVT).

Flash Coving & Hot Welding

Seamless, hygienic integration of floor and wall for healthcare and commercial environments using hot welding techniques.

Custom Pattern Finish

We specialize in hand-cut patterns including circles, ovals, curves, arcs, and spirals for unique design requirements.

Wall Protection

Installation of durable wall protection systems to prevent damage in high-traffic commercial corridors.

Consultation

Technical advice on material selection and installation methods suitable for your specific site conditions.
